What is VectorPunks?

VectorPunks is a collection of 100 unique characters inspired by the original CryptoPunks project by larva labs. They share similar attributes and archetype of different types used by the original cryptopunks. Only 100 of them will ever exist with 5 special punks that will also be minted. Supply for each punk will be 1/1. VectorPunks is not affiliated with Larva labs.
